Details

What is Dev, meet Tech?

Dev, meet Tech is an event for students addressing ways to use technology for positive development. About one third of the attendees will be from a technical background. The rest will be from a range of different disciplines with a range of different skillsets and outlooks on life. The one thing all attendees will have in common is their drive to be global citizens and in some way improve today’s world. After our speaker series in the morning, the cohort will be split into teams and spend the rest of the day developing a solution which uses technology to provoke a positive change of any size and any kind. We have some fantastic mentors to help guide the teams and an atmosphere of collaboration will be encouraged.

At the end of the day, the wonderful Zendesk will provide everyone with free pizzas and beers to reminisce on our productive developments and maybe even think about future plans :)

Is it free?

Yes, of course! Our hosts, Zendesk, will also be providing you with all the snacks and coffee that you can handle.

Alright, when?

Saturday 22nd August (detailed schedule to come).

Is it a competition?

No. The event is to be fueled on collaboration. All teams will be able to bounce off each other for ideas and help. There will be no overall winners, however, if teams would like to, there will be time for demos at the end of the day.

Why should I be a part of this?

“Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed citizens can change the world; indeed, it's the only thing that ever has.” - Margaret Mead

Who can apply?

Anyone who has an interest in social entrepreneurship or just making a positive contribution to this world. The baseline is that you should be able to contribute with something concrete – may it be in concept, pixels, experience, enthusiasm or something else. This event is targeted at students but we would be delighted to have any enthusiastic young people come along and participate with like minded change makers!

Who will be selected to participate?

We’re looking for people with their heart in the right place and great skills. We appreciate a genuine interest in making positive changes happen. While going through the applications, we will look for a passion for creating, sharing and learning. We would love team players and creators to apply - people who are open for building bridges and lighting fires.

What happens after I applied?

We will contact all applicants the week leading up the event to inform you on the layout of the day. We'll be starting at 10AM and have a wind down with pizza and drinks at 5PM.
